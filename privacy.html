🛡 Privacy Policy

Last Shelter — Pixel Survival RPG
Last updated: March 2026

1. Information We Collect

Last Shelter does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The game runs entirely on your device.

  • No account registration required
  • No personal data collected
  • No location data used
  • No camera or microphone access

2. Local Storage

Game progress (save slots, settings, language preference) is stored locally on your device only using browser localStorage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

3. In-App Purchases

In-app purchases are processed securely through Google Play Store. We do not collect or store your payment information. All transactions are handled by Google's payment system subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

4. Third-Party Services

This app uses Google Play Billing for in-app purchases. No other third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services are used.

5. Children's Privacy

Last Shelter does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The game contains no features that collect user data from any age group.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
